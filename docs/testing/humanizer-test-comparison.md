## AI Patterns Present in Original Text

The original text contains these common AI writing patterns (from Wikipedia's "Signs of AI writing" guide):

### Vocabulary
- "comprehensive", "robust", "crucial", "groundbreaking", "paradigm-shifting"
- "seamlessly", "meticulously", "thoroughly"
- "cutting-edge", "sophisticated"
- "leverages", "delves into", "encompasses"

### Structural Patterns
- "Additionally," / "Furthermore," / "Moreover," at start of sentences
- "It is important to note that..."
- Excessive bullet point adjectives ("Crucial Enhancement", "Significant Improvement")
- Rule of three (three items in lists)

### Tonal Issues
- Overly formal and promotional language
- Hedging phrases ("I believe")
- Inflated claims ("paradigm shift", "groundbreaking")
